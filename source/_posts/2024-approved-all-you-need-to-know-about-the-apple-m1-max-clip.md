### Apple M1 Max Specifications & Performance

* **CPU Cores:** 10
* **GPU Cores:** 24 or 32
* **Memory Bandwidth:** 400 Gbps

* **Maximum Memory:** 64 GB
* **RAM:** 32 Gigs
